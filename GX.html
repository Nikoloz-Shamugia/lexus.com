DESIGN

POWERFUL DESIGN

The all-new 2024 GX showcases an all-new exterior design, leading with a dynamic front fascia that continues the signature Lexus spindle body concept. A true body-on-frame SUV, the GX expresses its off-road prowess through its squared-off body lines, increased ground clearance and powerful stance. And for the first time ever, the GX features a standard power rear liftgate with a lift-out window and available kick sensor* for added convenience.

PERFORMANCE

TWIN-TURBOCHARGED POWER

The all-new GX features a highly capable and efficient twin-turbocharged 3.4L V6 engine paired with a 10-speed-Direct Shift automatic transmission. This engine has been engineered to produce an impressive 349 horsepower* and 479 lb-ft of torque* all while providing smooth and steady power delivery both on- and off-road. And for GX Premium, Premium+ and Overtrail grades equipped with the standard tow hitch, towing capacity has increased to 8,000 pounds.*

TECHNOLOGY & SAFETY

INTUITIVE TECHNOLOGY

Experience an interior where technology isn’t just second to none, it’s second nature. A standard 14-inch touchscreen display with Lexus Interface* works as an extension of you, enabling you to personalize your multimedia experience, and provides seamless, hands-free access to features like the available Mark Levinson®* Surround Sound, wireless Apple CarPlay®* integration and more.
